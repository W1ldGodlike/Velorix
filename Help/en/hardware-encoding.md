# Hardware acceleration (short)

Full automatic GPU picking and a dedicated tuning window are **still planned**. Today the editor relies on **software** encoders that run on any CPU and stay predictable.

## Why hardware will matter later

When support lands, hardware encoders help with **faster** drafts or bulk passes when a suitable GPU is present. Final “master” quality often still prefers software encoding.

## What to do now

Stay on the quality presets in the right rail and read the **command preview** at the bottom — it shows exactly what will hit disk.

See [ffmpeg-rail-presets.md](ffmpeg-rail-presets.md).
