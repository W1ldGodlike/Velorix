# Shortcuts and the menu

These accelerators are wired in the **main window menu** (labels follow the UI language).

- **Ctrl+O** or **Cmd+O** — open a video file in the editor.
- **Ctrl+Shift+Y** or **Cmd+Shift+Y** — switch to the **Downloads** shell route.
- **Ctrl+Shift+V** or **Cmd+Shift+V** — paste clipboard text into the Downloads queue.

## Paste outside text fields

When the caret is **not** inside a text field, **Ctrl+V** (or Cmd+V on macOS) with a URL-like clipboard payload may switch to **Downloads** and enqueue the URL — handy right after copying a link from a browser.
