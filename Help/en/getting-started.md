# FluxAlloy — quick start

1. Open the **Download** tab.
2. Paste URLs (one per line).
3. Click **Add to queue**, then **Start queue**.

To process local files, open the **Processing** tab, add files, and click **Run batch**. Social presets are covered in [processing-social-presets.md](processing-social-presets.md). Terminal, inspector, and watch folder: [tools-terminal-inspector.md](tools-terminal-inspector.md). GPU/CPU encoders: [hardware-encoding.md](hardware-encoding.md).
